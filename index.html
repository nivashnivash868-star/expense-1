Welcome to MyFin!

We're excited to help you take control of your financial journey. Let's walk through a brief 1-minute setup guide to show you around the premium dashboard!

Step 1: Setup Your Bank Accounts

Go to the Accounts tab. Register Checking accounts, Savings vaults, or Credit cards. When you record transactions, your ledger balances synchronize automatically! You can also execute instant internal transfers.

Step 2: Log Income & Expenses

Add records by clicking Add Transaction. Classify under categories (Food, Transport, Rent) to monitor where your money goes. The active views let you filter and view records by category and dates.

Step 3: Scan to Pay & Show QR

Visit the Scan to Pay page to execute simulated QR/UPI payments to retail partner outlets. Want to receive money? Switch to the **Show My QR** tab, choose an account, and display your dynamic deposit QR code!

Step 4: AI Financial Advisor

Need tips? Click the AI Advisor tab. The app monitors your budget burn rate, calculates monthly savings, and provides real-time recommendation alerts to limit losses and boost cashflows.
